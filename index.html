Settings & setup
Step 1 · Load your Google Timeline
Your photos lost their GPS, but Google Maps knows where you were. Export your Timeline from Google Maps and load it here. The file stays on your device — never uploaded.
Required for location magic

From Android Settings:

  1. Open Settings on your phone
  2. Tap Location
  3. Tap Location services (or scroll down)
  4. Tap Timeline (sometimes shown as "Google Location History" or "Your Timeline")
  5. Tap Export Timeline data
  6. Save the JSON file, then load it here

Alternative — from Google Maps: Profile picture (top right) → Your Timeline → ⋮ menu → Location and privacy settings → Export Timeline data.

The file is called Timeline.json or location-history.json. Both work.

Step 2 · Anthropic API key
Needed so Claude can identify specific places (Shell Island, that restaurant you were at) from coordinates. Stored only on your device. Get a key at console.anthropic.com/settings/keys.
Step 3 · Google Places API key
Lets the app find specific businesses, parks, and attractions (Frank Brown Park, Goofy Golf) from your coordinates. Stored only on your device.
